Ruslan Lunev wins Gold in 10m Air Pistol at ISSF Granada

Azerbaijan's Ruslan Lunev claimed gold in the 10m Air Pistol Men event at the ISSF competition in Granada, Spain, delivering a composed performance against a strong international field.

22 May, 2022

The opening day of finals at the ISSF competition in Granada, Spain brought excitement across all 10m individual events, but it was Azerbaijan's Ruslan Lunev who delivered the standout performance of the day. Lunev claimed gold in the 10m Air Pistol Men event, showcasing a confident and composed display on the international stage.

A winner and medalist of the ISSF Grand Prix, Lunev once again proved his class in a tense final. He held off strong competition, including Tokyo 2020 Olympic silver medalist Damir Mikec of Serbia, who finished second. Bronze went to South Korea's Wonho Lee, marking an impressive debut at ISSF level.

Despite strong performances across all events, the highlight of the day remained Ruslan Lunev's victory — a significant achievement for Azerbaijan on the international stage.

“This result reflects the dedication of our athletes and coaching staff. We are proud to represent Azerbaijan at the highest level.”

Coaches also highlighted the importance of long-term athlete development, emphasizing the federation’s investment in youth programs and international preparation camps.
